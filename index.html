Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is the course duration?

Course duration depends on the batch you choose. JEE batches typically run for 12-18 months, while board focused batches are 6-12 months. NEET batches are customized based on your preparation level.

Do you provide online classes?

Yes! We provide both online and offline classes. Our online classes are live, interactive, and recorded for later reference. You can attend from anywhere with internet connectivity.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our teaching quality. No questions asked. Your satisfaction is our priority.

What if I miss a class?

All classes are recorded and available in the student portal. You can watch recorded lectures anytime. Plus, you can attend the same lecture in any other batch.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, comprehensive study materials including notes, previous year papers, important formulas, and question banks are provided for all courses.

How do I get admission?

Simply fill the registration form, attend a free demo class, and enroll. No entrance exam required. You can start immediately after enrollment.
