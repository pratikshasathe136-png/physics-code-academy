About Physics Code Academy

Building future scientists, one concept at a time

Why Physics Code?

Physics Code Academy is built around one clear promise: teach physics with logic, not rote memorization. Under direct mentorship of Srujan Sir, we help 11th and 12th grade students prepare for JEE, NEET, MHT-CET and board exams with smart strategy and personalized support.

Programs for 11th & 12th Grade

  • JEE (Main + Advanced): Deep concept building + advanced numericals
  • MHT-CET (90-99%): Fast-solving techniques and MCQ practice
  • NEET Physics: Strong theory with MCQ mastery and NCERT focus
  • Boards (CBSE/HSC): Clear theory, answer writing practice and PYQ training

What You Get

  • Concept clarity from basics to advanced
  • Premium chapter-wise study material
  • Handwritten + digital notes
  • DPP (Daily Practice Problems)
  • PYQs with detailed discussion
  • Weekly tests and performance analysis
  • Dedicated doubt solving

Session Types Available
